About

Capella Days regularly brings together the community of Capella and Arcadia:

  • creators of this innovative systems engineering solution,
  • providers of Capella add-ons and services,
  • MBSE experts and industrial users.

Capella Days is your opportunity to learn from Capella ecosystem members!

Benefit from valuable best practices and industrial feedback based on real-world applications.

Call for proposals

Submit a talk for Capella Days 2025.

This year, the program will feature two main tracks:

  • Industrial Feedback
  • Best Practices

We also welcome proposals for alternative session formats (e.g. tutorials, demos, panels).
Don’t hesitate to share your ideas—we’d be happy to discuss how they could fit into the event.
