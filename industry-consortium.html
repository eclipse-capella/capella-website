The Capella IC hosts the Eclipse Capella™ ecosystem stakeholders
in a vendor neutral way with an open governance

Mission

To fulfill the overall MBSE vision, Capella tool associated with Arcadia method offers a mature, full featured, sustainable solution which can be widely diffused thanks to an open source alternative to existing proprietary modeling tools.

Capella ecosystem is made up vibrant stakeholders who need to collaborate in a vendor neutral way with an open governance:

  • companies developing complex systems and softwares
  • suppliers providing offers on the top of Capella
  • academics with a MBSE topic for research need

The Capella Industry Consortium (IC) aims to offer to its members a place to:

  • foster collaborations,
  • share investment and best practices,
  • leverage Open Innovation to co-develop new MBSE capabilities.

Goals

Capella IC provides to its members an open governance on several axis related to Capella tool, add-ons and underlying technologies:

  • Knowledge Sharing: case studies, technology watch, private exchange workshops on N&N
  • Promotion: Promote Capella as a leading solution, Provide material to executive
  • Product management: Requirements co-creation, Discuss the roadmap
  • Development of the Community: collaboration between research/academia, suppliers, end-users
  • Joint development financing: Coordinate investments to reduce, development time, risks, and cost, and maximize ROI

The Capella IC activity is driven by several committees: the Steering Committee,
the Product Management Committee and the Marketing Committee.

Membership

The membership classes of the Capella IC are designed to reflect the varied interests of the members.


Driver Members

Influence the development of Capella and be an integral part of the group of organizations that govern the development of the open source MBSE solution.


Participant Members

Contribute to the development and adoption of Capella: development of viewpoints, participation at conferences, providing user experiences to end-users, etc.

Research/Academia Members

Universities and research organizations that want to participate in the development of the overall MBSE solution based on Capella.

Wether you are an end-user, a tool provider or a researcher
you can also be part of this vibrant ecosystem!
