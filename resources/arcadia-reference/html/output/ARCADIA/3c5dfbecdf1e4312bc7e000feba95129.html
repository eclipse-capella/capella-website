Interactions between operational activities are formalised through operational interactions.

Each interaction links one operational activityto another.

The description of activities and interactions linking them is known as operational dataflow.

The nature of what is exchanged between two activities is expressed as one (or several) exchange items. Each of them is linked to one or several interactions carrying them. An exchange item references data of the global data model.
