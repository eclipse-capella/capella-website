IVVQ Strategy

Engineering Data

Major Kinds of Engineering Data > IVV > IVV MASTERING > IVVQ Strategy

The justified set of all steps in IVVQ and the required enablers and prerequisites of each step: configuration items, expected functional contents, workflow/order of assembly and of integration, enablins systems and test means, etc.

Justification relies notably on dependencies between required elements, customer and industrial value analysis. The strategy may be reconsidered according to IVV ups and downs, delays, difficulties, and so on.

Activities producing the Data

  • Define a Components IVVQ Strategy
  • Formalise Component development & integration contract
  • Plan & manage IVVQ

Activities using the Data

  • Build IVV Test Suites and campaigns
  • Select and validate best compromise Architecture
  • Perform Sub-System Engineering
  • Perform HW & mechanical engineering
  • Build PIDS documents (Prime Item Development Specification) and subsystems, SW, HW specifications (SSS, SRS, HRS) including interfaces
  • Perform SW engineering
  • Formalise Component development & integration contract
  • Build IVVQ documents (Integration, Verification, Validation, Qualification)
  • Build Test Means & enabling Systems
