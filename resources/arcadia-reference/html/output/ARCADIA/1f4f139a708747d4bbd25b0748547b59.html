Product Line Definition

Engineering Process

Engineering Capabilities > Minimise Development Cost > Product Line Definition

This process describes the way to build the solution and the product line jointly.

The Product Line main contents (market segmentation, portfolio description, resulting variabilities on solution) is supported and shaped by the need and solution descriptions.

In turn, the product line variabilities have to be applied to all engineering assets including architecture description. The architecture may have to evolve according to variability constraints, in an iterative manner.
