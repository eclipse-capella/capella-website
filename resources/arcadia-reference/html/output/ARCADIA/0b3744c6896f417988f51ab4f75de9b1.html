Agility in Engineering Course

Engineering Process

Engineering Capabilities > Support Collaboration in Engineering > Agility in Engineering Course

This process describes the way to incrementally define solution and design/develop subsystems , notably in an agile software cycle context.

The definition of the solution may be iterative and/or incremental, driven by parts of physical architecture to be implemented at each iteration or increment: components, functional chains etc.

In case of software agile development, the physical architecture drives the global consistency of developments and maintains a vision at termination. The contents of each sprint is based on capabilities, functional chains and scenarios. Each sprint result may lead to modifythe vision of architecture at termination.
