How to realise Engineering Capabilities a short Guidance



This figure lists some major engineering capabilities and concerns or challenges, and the way Arcadia suggests to address them, under the form of workflows (or processes) traversing the former activities and creating or exploiting former data.

These workflows orchestrate Arcadia activities described in other figures. The reader is invited to refer to the description of each activity prior to considering these workflows.

For each activity involved in a workflow, the specifics of its contribution to the workflow are described in the workflow context here. They complement the description of the activity.
