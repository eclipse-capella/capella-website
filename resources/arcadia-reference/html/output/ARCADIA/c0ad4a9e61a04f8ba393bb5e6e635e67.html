IVV Releases

Engineering Data

Major Kinds of Engineering Data > IVV > IVV MASTERING > IVV Releases

The description of the system contents of a step in the IVV flow, according to the IVV strategy, in terms of required components, functional contents and non functional properties/performances

Activities producing the Data

  • Define a Components IVVQ Strategy
  • Define customer deliveries based on operational capabilities
  • Formalise Component development & integration contract
  • Plan & manage IVVQ

Activities using the Data

  • Build IVV Test Suites and campaigns
  • Perform Verification
  • Select and validate best compromise Architecture
  • Perform Sub-System Engineering
  • Perform HW & mechanical engineering
  • Perform Integration
  • Plan & manage IVVQ
  • Perform Solution Validation
  • Build PIDS documents (Prime Item Development Specification) and subsystems, SW, HW specifications (SSS, SRS, HRS) including interfaces
  • Perform SW engineering
  • Formalise Component development & integration contract
  • Justify requirements fulfilment
  • Build IVVQ documents (Integration, Verification, Validation, Qualification)
  • Build Test Means & enabling Systems

Usages

  • Validation plan
  • Integration and verification plan
  • IVVQ Strategy
  • Delivery
