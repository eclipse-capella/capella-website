Modes & States FOP 1 Define operational Modes & States

A mode or state characterizes the behaviour of an actor, user... in given environmental conditions. A mode or state is activated by external conditions and/or by users.

Only some paths (named transitions) are allowed between modes or states, usually under internal or external conditions.

A mode or state may specify which activities are available or not during this mode or state.
