About

PRFC is a Consulting and Training company specialized in MBSE (Model-Based Systems Engineering).

PRFC was founded by Pascal Roques, senior consultant with more than 25 years of experience in the field and author of several books on modeling with Arcadia/Capella, SysML and UML.

Training

PRFC offers MBSE training courses. These courses are aimed at all types of profiles, and can range from a technical overview of the Arcadia method to the modeling of realistic case studies with the associated Capella tool.
